Strawberry Lime Smoothies

Peak-of-freshness strawberries make this thinner, easy drink a summer staple. 

Total Time
Prep/Total Time: 5 min.

Makes 3 servings

Ingredients

1 cup strawberry yogurt
1/2 cup 2% milk
2 to 4 tablespoons lime juice
2 tablespoons honey
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled

Directions
Process all ingredients in a covered blender until smooth.
Nutrition Facts
1 cup: 172 calories, 2g fat (1g saturated fat), 7mg cholesterol, 61mg sodium, 36g carbohydrate (32g sugars, 2g fiber), 5g protein.
